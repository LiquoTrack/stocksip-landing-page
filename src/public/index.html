Frequently Asked Questions

How does this app help me manage my liquor store? ▼
Our app allows you to keep real-time inventory control, record sales, receive low stock alerts, and optimize your orders to suppliers with intelligent analytics.
Can I receive notifications when a product runs out? ▼
Yes, you will receive automatic alerts when a product is about to run out, allowing you to make timely decisions and avoid costly stockouts.
Does the app allow generating sales and stock reports? ▼
Absolutely! Generate detailed reports on sales performance, profit margins, best-selling products, and comprehensive inventory turnover analysis.
Is it difficult to set up the app for my business? ▼
Not at all! The setup is straightforward and intuitive. We also provide comprehensive support and tutorials to get you started quickly.
